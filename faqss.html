  What is the difference between 1-megapixel and 2-megapixel cameras?

1-megapixel camera is HD 720p

2-megapixel camera is full HD 1080p

Where is your site?

I want cameras without wires

For indoor cameras ... (not exposed to sun and rain)
There is a small camera (apple) that can be connected to Wi-Fi directly and watch from the mobile. 2-megapixel, 360-degree daytime night vision, microphone to hear from around, and speakers to talk. For SAR 500 including delivery, installation and configuration settings without memory. With the installation of memory 64 GB 150 riyals to be total 650 riyals.
Notes:

1- Memory 64 GB records for up to 4 days continuously 24 hours, after that the old recordings are automatically deleted and new recording is done.

2- The camera cannot be installed on the ceiling but is placed on a desk or a bookshelf or can be installed by the wall.

For external cameras ... (exposed to sun and rain) {none}

How many meters of wires will I get for each camera?

For each camera you will get a 10 meters wire, an additional meter can be added for 5 riyals.

Are there any other amounts to be paid?

1. VAT on the total amount of the invoice 5%

2. The number of meters in addition of the default meters that come with each camera. (for each additional meter 5 riyals)

3. If the workers used decorations during installation to cover the internal wires, 7 riyals will be charged for each 3 meters.

4. Default HD cable comes with one and a half meter to connect from the recorder of the screen, additional charges for a longer cable.

What is the warranty period?

Warranty for all basic parts of the system is complete two years. We provide an advantage that the warranty contains replacement of the piece instead of repairing it. The warranty period starts from the invoice date.

Extension wires will distort the parallax, are there solutions?

Usually technician looks at the wires in the walls, if there are extensions that can be installed on it is good. Otherwise, the technician and his assistants will have to make new extensions from the beginning. Taking coverage of external and internal wires and passing it appropriately. The technician will tell the owner before making any hole in the wall.

Do I have a screen?

Technicians will install the monitoring system on any screen in the place, be it a computer monitor or a TV screen. If you do not have a monitor, you can buy a 19-inch or 20-inch (computer monitor) for 300 riyals and attach it to the system. If you have an internet connection, we can connect the cameras to the mobile and you can do without the screen.

Will I be able to view the cameras from the phone?

Yes, it is possible but an internet connection is required where the cameras will be installed.
Note:
1. If the existing internet is a fixed modem, the internet cord will be extended from the modern to the recording device.

2. For the distance from modem to the recorder, you can use a network booster of 150 riyals to make a wireless connection.

3. We prefer a modem or a fixed router at home and not Wi-Fi as a strong internet so that there are no problems with vision
