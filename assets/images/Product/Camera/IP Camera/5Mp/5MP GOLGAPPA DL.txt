Hikvision DS-2CD2755FWD-IZS 5MP

Key Features
2944 x 1656 Resolution at 20 fps
Up to 100' of Night Vision
2.8-12mm Varifocal Lens
88-29° Horizontal Field of View
The DS-2CD2755FWD-IZS 5MP Outdoor Network Dome Camera from Hikvision has a 5MP 1/2.9" progressive scan CMOS sensor to capture 2944 x 1656 resolution video at 20 fps. The 2.8-12mm varifocal lens features an 88-29° horizontal field of view. EXIR technology delivers up to 100' of night vision. This camera has an audio input and output for two way audio support.
